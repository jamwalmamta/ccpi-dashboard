Climate change is one of the most pressing challenges of the 21st century. Countries worldwide are adopting diverse strategies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, expand renewable energy usage, improve energy efficiency, and strengthen climate policies.

The CCPI Analysis System transforms Climate Change Performance Index data into meaningful insights using data analytics, visualization, and machine learning. The system enables year-wise analysis, country comparisons, trend evaluation, and future performance prediction.
